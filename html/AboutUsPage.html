Jockey'sConferenceCenter

The Jockeys Conference Center is a premier venue located in a picturesque setting that caters to conferences, meetings, and events. Our center is dedicated to providing a professional and comfortable environment for various gatherings, from corporate conferences to social events.

At the Jockeys Conference Center, we understand the importance of creating the perfect atmosphere for productive and successful events. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with modern amenities and technology to ensure seamless presentations, discussions, and networking opportunities.

The Jockeys Conference Center traces its origins back to the 19th century when it was originally established as the Jockeys Manor, an elegant countryside estate. Built by a wealthy aristocrat with a passion for horse racing, the manor quickly became a hub for equestrian enthusiasts and social gatherings.


Over the years, the Jockeys Manor gained a reputation for hosting lavish parties, horse racing events, and exclusive galas. Its sprawling grounds, with meticulously maintained gardens and picturesque landscapes, created an enchanting atmosphere that captivated visitors.
As the manor's popularity grew, it became apparent that there was a demand for a dedicated space to host conferences and meetings amidst the natural beauty and tranquil surroundings. Recognizing this opportunity, the owners decided to expand the estate and transform it into the Jockeys Conference Center.
The transformation involved carefully preserving the historical charm of the manor while incorporating modern facilities and amenities suitable for hosting business events. The center was designed to seamlessly blend old-world elegance with contemporary functionality.
Over the years, the Jockeys Conference Center has evolved into a premier destination for conferences, corporate retreats, and special events. Its rich history and stately ambiance continue to attract visitors from around the world.
Today, the Jockeys Conference Center stands as a symbol of timeless grandeur and sophistication. Its historic roots, coupled with its commitment to providing top-notch service and state-of-the-art facilities, make it a sought-after venue for those seeking a unique and memorable event experience.
